# Product Requirements Document: SmartHome Energy Manager

## Overview
A comprehensive energy management system for households designed to help consumers reduce energy costs and carbon footprint through real-time monitoring and AI-powered optimization.

## Product Name
EnergyFlow Pro

## Product Description
An IoT-enabled energy management platform that monitors household consumption patterns and automatically optimizes appliance usage. Features predictive analytics for demand forecasting and integration with renewable energy sources.

## Core Features
- Real-time Energy Consumption Monitoring
- Predictive Analytics Dashboard
- Smart Appliance Integration
- Solar Integration Support
- Mobile App Control
- AI-Powered Optimization Engine
- Carbon Footprint Tracking
- Energy Cost Predictions

## Pricing Information
Base Model: ₹8,500
Premium Model: ₹12,500
Enterprise Package: ₹25,000+

## Pricing Strategy
Tiered pricing strategy with premium positioning for feature-rich variants. Emphasis on ROI through energy savings.

## Target Segments
- Middle to upper-middle-income households (₹60K-150K monthly income)
- Environmentally conscious consumers
- Early adopters of smart home technology

## Geographic Focus
Primary: India (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai)
Secondary: Southeast Asia expansion planned for 2026

## Market Context
Growing awareness of climate change and rising electricity costs (15% YoY increase) driving demand for energy-efficient solutions. Government incentives for renewable energy adoption. Smart meter penetration at 25% and growing rapidly in metros.
