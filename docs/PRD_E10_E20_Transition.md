| Field | Value |

### 2. Strategic Rationale (The E20 Transition)

The Indian government's accelerated mandate for E20 fuel (20% ethanol) poses a catastrophic mechanical risk to legacy vehicles. Ethanol's hygroscopic nature leads to phase separation, brass/copper corrosion, and rapid degradation of rubber fuel lines, creating extreme fire hazards. **Etha-Guard Nexus** provides a hybrid hardware/software/chemical solution to "E20-proof" any older vehicle without a massive OEM service bill.

### 3. Core Features

**F1. Etha-Guard Pro Additive (Recurring)**
- Chemical stabilizer formulated to neutralize acidity and prevent water-ethanol phase separation in the fuel tank.
- Delivered monthly via a direct-to-consumer subscription.

**F2. Retrofit Hardware Kit (One-time)**
- High-grade, ethanol-resistant fluoroelastomer fuel lines.
- Zinc/brass component replacements tailored to specific classic and budget vehicle models.

**F3. Companion App & Diagnostic Sensor**
- Cheap OBD-II plug that monitors fuel mixture health and alerts the user if water concentration approaches critical engine-misfire thresholds.

### 4. Target Market & Personas

**Persona A: The Daily Auto-Rickshaw Driver**
- *Pain Point:* Livelihood depends on an older vehicle. Cannot afford engine replacement or expensive OEM kits.
- *Nexus Solution:* The ₹499/mo chemical additive to keep the engine running cheaply. 

**Persona B: Classic/Legacy Car Enthusiast**
- *Pain Point:* Possesses a 1990s vintage vehicle where OEM E20 kits do not exist. Fears total engine loss.
- *Nexus Solution:* The ₹4,999 premium retrofit hardware kit.
